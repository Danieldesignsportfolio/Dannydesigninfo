Who I Am

The Story Behind
the Frames

I'm Daniel Praveen Bejjipalli — a Creative Video Editor and Motion Graphics Designer based in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. I started my journey in 2018 with a passion for brand storytelling and visual communication, and I've never looked back.

What began at Oppo Mobiles as an Assistant Branding Manager shaped my understanding of how visuals drive perception. Moving into video editing and motion graphics, I grew through Sanghamitra Media where I managed full production pipelines — from shoot planning to final delivery.

"Every frame is a decision. Every cut is an intention. I don't just edit videos — I craft experiences that make people stop scrolling and start feeling."

Today, at Adbuffs Media, I create high-converting ad creatives and motion graphics optimised for performance marketing. Working under tight deadlines across multiple brand campaigns, I've developed a discipline for fast, high-quality output without compromising creative depth.

My work combines strong storytelling, sharp typography, fluid motion design, and cinematic colour grading — all in service of one goal: content that converts and captivates.
